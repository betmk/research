Mitigating Factors & Flow Rate Constraints

Announced relief ≠ delivered barrels. Physical deployment rates are constrained by pipeline bottlenecks, terminal capacity, and logistics. The paper balance sheet and physical market diverge significantly.

  • Yanbu throughput (latest): ~3.9 mb/d (LSEG/Kpler, week of Mar 30) — down 15% from peak of ~4.6 mb/d (week of Mar 23). Port at ~78% of ~5 mb/d max.
  • Net new export: +3.15 mb/d (3.9 mb/d current minus 750K pre-crisis). Duration: indefinite. Limitation: crude only.
  • ⚠ Apr 8: IRGC drone hit pipeline pumping station. Bloomberg: damage limited, flows continued, Yanbu loadings not interrupted.

Repair & Restart Timelines

Shut-in Wells (Well Damage) STRUCTURAL

  • 38+ days idle as of Apr 10. Water coning risk active since Mar 28 in mature Gulf reservoirs (Iraq Rumaila, Saudi, Kuwait).
